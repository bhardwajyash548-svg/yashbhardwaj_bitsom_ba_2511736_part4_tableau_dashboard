# Executive Dashboard Story

## Executive Summary

The dashboard provides leadership with a consolidated view of sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, shipping efficiency, and discount effectiveness.

The business is performing well overall with strong profitability and stable monthly sales trends.

## What Is Performing Well

- The South region is the strongest contributor to sales.
- Technology products are generating the highest profit.
- The Home Office segment contributes significantly to revenue.
- Monthly sales remain stable across the year.

## What Is Underperforming

- Standard Class shipping experiences longer delivery times.
- High discounts negatively affect profitability.
- Some categories exhibit higher return risk.

## Risks Identified

- Excessive discounting can erode margins.
- Shipping delays may impact customer satisfaction.
- Dependence on a few profitable categories increases business concentration risk.

## Opportunities Identified

- Expand high-margin technology offerings.
- Increase investment in high-performing regions.
- Develop personalized campaigns for Home Office customers.

## Recommended Actions

1. Optimize discount policies.
2. Improve shipping efficiency.
3. Expand profitable product categories.
4. Increase investment in high-performing regions.

## Dashboard Limitations

- External market factors are not included.
- Customer lifetime value is not available.
- Competitor information is unavailable.

## Suggested Next Analysis

- Customer churn analysis.
- Product recommendation modeling.
- Forecasting future sales trends.
- Cohort analysis for customer retention.
